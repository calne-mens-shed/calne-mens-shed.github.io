Latest News

I thought it time to attempt an update as to what is going on at Calne Men’s Shed since the start of the lockdown.

I guess the short answer is very little, in the Shed itself that is. However we are all in contact using emails and WhatsApp not to forget, the good old fashioned telephone. Many of the members are carrying out any number of useful tasks, from making PPE for front line workers, to things to sell when we can return to a more normal existence, such as bug sheds bird boxes etc. Life goes on.
