Latest News

Although a meaningful update has been long overdue, at long last I am delighted to tell you all that the Calne Men’s Shed has been able to re – open.

As I’m sure you will have noticed from elsewhere in this new letter, our current opening hours are 13.00 to 17.00 on Tuesdays and Thursdays and although we are initially restricting attendance to six members per session, we hope to increase both the days and times on which we open, depending on the demand, thus allowing more of you to attend.

We currently operate a first come first served booking system, which seems to be working well – please contact me on - enquiries@calnemensshed.org - if you would like to make a booking.

May I suggest that if you would like to just see what we are all about before possibly joining, then a good time to look in is just as we are starting a session, (13.00) or as we are finishing, (17.00) as we normally less congested at these times.

Finally I am pleased to tell you that we have no shortage of new projects to get your teeth into.

Current opening hours:
Tuesdays & Thursdays - 13.00 to 17.00
We will add to these times when Covid restrictions allow.
